Brr Brr Patapim

Brr Brr Patapim is a towering fusion of tree and baboon, combining brute strength with wild agility.
With bark-like skin and powerful limbs, he overwhelms opponents with his crushing Brr Brr Barrage
and unpredictable strikes. Patapim fights with the raw, primal force of nature, leaving his foes no chance to escape the chaos.
