Bombardiro Crocodilo

Bombardiro Crocodilo enters the IBFC with a style as unique as his appearance. With a background in precision
striking and long-range pressure tactics, Bombardiro excels at controlling distance and dictating the pace of a match from the outside.
Built like a tank with reach to match, he uses wide-arcing strikes, explosive lunges, and unorthodox movement to keep opponents off balance.
Where others close in—he stays just out of reach, landing heavy shots and retreating before a response can come.He’s a fighter that blends
strategy with spectacle—calculated chaos, delivered from above.
